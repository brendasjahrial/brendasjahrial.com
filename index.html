Hello, I’m Brenda, a strategist based in London. Currently working at dn&co on place-branding. Previously at Nice and Serious.

I grew up in Jakarta and Singapore, before coming to London to study advertising at UAL. Since my degree, I’ve worked on campaigns and brand strategies for innocent, Nando’s, Macmillan and WWF.

I love researching and getting immersed in different worlds, finding new ways of looking at things and turning them into compelling stories.

Selected work

  • Start Network. Positively disrupting the humanitarian aid system.
  • Breast Cancer Now. Showing the uncomfortable reality of incurable breast cancer.
  • Semble. Capturing the endless energy of community projects around the UK.
  • Lift the Ban. Bringing together more than 200 organisations for a common cause.
  • Refugee Action. Creating a brand inspired by the unrelenting spirit of refugees and people seeking asylum.
